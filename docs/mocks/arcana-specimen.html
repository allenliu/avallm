The AvaLLM Arcana

A full tarot anatomy for every card the game deals: numeral cartouche, engraved emblem, title plate, corner stars. Roles become named arcana; quest results become The Sun and The Tower; votes are cards you play. Hover any card.

The Roles

Your secret identity, dealt face-down at game start and flipped in the reveal. Numerals nod to the classical deck — Death is XIII, The Moon is XVIII, The Devil is XV, The Hermit is IX.

Fortunes of the Quest

The quest line is a five-card spread. Future quests lie face-down; the current one glows; a resolved quest flips to its fortune — The Sun for success, The Tower for ruin. The flip is the game's core animation.

Where the deck shows up:
  • Role reveal — your arcana is dealt face-down at game start and flips on tap. The endgame reveal deals all seven face-down in seat order, then flips them one by one.
  • Quest resolution — the current face-down card flips to The Sun or The Tower; the number of fail cards appears on the title plate.
  • Voting — you play Approve or Reject from your hand; the public tally lays everyone's played cards in a row (a natural "vote record" visual).
  • Reversed cards (future idea) — in tarot, a reversed card inverts its meaning. Long-press a seat to mark your private suspicion: their card renders upside-down for you alone.
  • Emblem system — every emblem is a 60×60 stroke-only SVG, so they recolor with the palette and scale from 12px chips to full cards.
