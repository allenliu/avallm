Sealed the same way — revealed differently

Votes and quest cards both seal as a ballot. But votes are public and attributed once revealed, while quest cards are anonymous — only the number of fails is ever shown. So the two ballots share the sealing stage and diverge at resolution. Here's the shared stage, then three ways the quest ballot can resolve without leaking who failed.

Q1 · Gather & shuffle → aggregateThe sealed cards slide into a single stack and shuffle — then the stack flips ONCE to the quest result (The Sun / The Tower + fail count). Individual cards are never shown. This is exactly how the physical game preserves anonymity: collect, shuffle, reveal only the count.
