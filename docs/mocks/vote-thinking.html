Sealed ballots, not typing indicators

The ghost row is right for discussion (agents speak one at a time). But a vote is secret and simultaneous — everyone decides at once — so per-bot "is thinking" rows pile up and leak the moment. Treatments for the vote/quest phases only; discussion keeps its ghost row.

Today — the problemEvery pending voter gets its own ghost row, so the feed stacks up while you decide, and it reads like a chat where five people are typing at once.

B · The live ballotA face-down card per voter, pending (hatched, pulsing) until that seat locks, then flipping face-up as cast — the sealed ballots filling in real time. Becomes the vote tally when complete. Progress you can watch without revealing anyone's choice.
